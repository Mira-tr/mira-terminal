# Foundations

The Design System protects clarity.

Every visual choice must help people understand:

- What this is.
- Where they are.
- What is important.
- What they can do.
- What is safe.

## Visual Hierarchy

Hierarchy must come from:

- Content order.
- Typography.
- Spacing.
- Surface.
- Border.
- State.

Do not rely on color alone.

## Surfaces

| Surface | Purpose | Density |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Brand Public | Explain RELMUA and route visitors. | Low to medium. |
| Creator Public | Show creator identity and work. | Medium. |
| Studio / Admin | Edit, validate, build, and operate. | Medium to high. |

## Content Density

Brand Public:

- More white space.
- Fewer controls.
- One main next action.

Creator Public:

- More personality.
- Clear creator navigation.
- Enough detail to understand activity.

Studio / Admin:

- Compact panels.
- Clear status.
- Fast repeated input.
- No oversized marketing layout.

## State Hierarchy

States must be visible:

- Default.
- Hover.
- Active.
- Focus-visible.
- Selected.
- Disabled.
- Loading.
- Success.
- Warning.
- Error.
- Empty.
- Visited.

Focus-visible must never be removed.

Disabled items must not look clickable.

## Surface Rule

Do not copy Public spacing into Studio.

Do not copy Studio density into Public.

Do not make Creator Public look like Brand Public with a different title.
