# Danger Zone

## Purpose

Danger Zone separates risky operations from normal work.

## Applies To

- Delete.
- Reset.
- Import execution.
- Restore.
- Log Clear.
- Delete all data.
- Publish.

## Required Information

- What will change.
- Affected items.
- Count.
- Backup recommendation.
- Whether undo or rollback exists.

## Interaction

- Confirm.
- Cancel.
- Focus trap for modal.
- Escape closes when safe.
- Focus returns after close.
- Undo or rollback shown when available.

## Visual Rules

- Separate from normal actions.
- Use danger token.
- Do not place next to primary save without spacing.
- Do not use color alone.

## Studio

Danger Zone is most common in Studio.

It must stay compact but unmistakable.
