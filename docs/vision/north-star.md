# RELMUA North Star

RELMUA exists to help creative work continue.

It is a brand that gathers works, tools, notes, creators, and personal creative
domains into a system that can be maintained for years without losing its
reason.

Studio is the desk.

Public is the gallery.

Creator Sites are independent rooms.

Data is the archive.

Validation is the gate.

Backup is the safety net.

Migration is the promise that old work will not be abandoned.

The goal is not to publish more pages.

The goal is to make RELMUA easier to grow, easier to trust, and easier to return
to ten years from now.

When a decision is unclear, ask:

> Does this make RELMUA easier to grow as a brand ten years from now?
